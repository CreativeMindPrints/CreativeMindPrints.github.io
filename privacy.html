1. About Creative Mind Prints

Creative Mind Prints is a digital products business specializing in planners, journals, templates, digital art, and printables. This privacy policy governs our Pinterest automation application used for marketing our digital products.

2. Pinterest Bot Application Purpose

The Creative Mind Prints Pinterest Bot is designed to:

  • Automate Pinterest pin creation for Creative Mind Prints products
  • Generate SEO-optimized pin content using local AI technology
  • Organize pins into appropriate Creative Mind Prints boards
  • Maintain consistent branding across all Pinterest marketing

3. Information We Access

The Creative Mind Prints Pinterest Bot accesses only:

  • Pinterest Account Data: Your Pinterest boards, pins, and basic profile information
  • Creative Mind Prints Product Files: Digital products you want to market on Pinterest
  • Local System Data: Basic performance metrics stored locally on your computer

4. What We DON'T Collect

We explicitly do not collect, store, or access:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone, email)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Private messages or communications
  • Browser history or personal data from other apps
  • Any data from other applications or services

5. Local-Only Data Processing

Privacy-First Commitment: All data processing happens locally on your computer. We never store your personal data on external servers or in the cloud.

6. How We Use Information

Creative Mind Prints uses accessed information solely to:

  • Generate Pinterest marketing pins for your Creative Mind Prints products
  • Post content to your Pinterest boards with proper categorization
  • Provide basic performance analytics for your Pinterest marketing
  • Maintain Creative Mind Prints brand consistency across all pins

7. Data Security

Creative Mind Prints implements robust security measures:

  • Local AI Processing: No cloud AI services, all processing on your computer
  • Encrypted Storage: Pinterest API credentials stored locally in encrypted files
  • Minimal Data Transmission: Only pin content sent to Pinterest (normal usage)
  • Regular Updates: Continuous security updates for all components

8. Third-Party Services

The Creative Mind Prints Pinterest Bot interacts with:

  • Pinterest API: For creating and managing pins (subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy)
  • Ollama AI: Local AI models running on your computer (no external data transmission)

9. Your Rights and Control

As a Creative Mind Prints Pinterest Bot user, you have complete control:

  • Full Ownership: You own all generated content and pins
  • Local Data Access: All data stored locally on your computer
  • Easy Removal: Uninstall the bot to remove all associated data
  • Pinterest Control: Revoke app access anytime in Pinterest account settings

10. Changes to This Policy

Creative Mind Prints may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date above
  • Significant changes will be posted on this page
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
