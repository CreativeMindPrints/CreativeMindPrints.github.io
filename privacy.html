1. About Creative Mind Prints

Creative Mind Prints is a financial wellness business specializing in printable budget planners, debt trackers, savings tools, and expense tracking printables. This privacy policy covers both our digital store and our personal-use Pinterest automation bot.

2. Pinterest Bot (Personal Use)

The Creative Mind Prints Pinterest Bot is used personally by Creative Mind Prints to:

  • Automate Pinterest pin creation for our financial printables
  • Generate SEO-optimized pin content using local AI technology
  • Organize pins into appropriate Creative Mind Prints boards
  • Maintain consistent branding across our Pinterest marketing

Note: This bot is for Creative Mind Prints internal use only and does not access customer data.

3. Customer Information (Store)

For our digital store, we may collect:

  • Purchase Information: Email address, name, and payment details (processed by payment provider)
  • Download Activity: When and what products you download
  • Communication: Messages you send us for support

4. Pinterest Bot Data Handling

Privacy-First Bot: Our Pinterest automation bot processes all data locally. No customer or personal data is shared with the bot system.

The Pinterest bot only accesses:

  • Creative Mind Prints Pinterest account (our business account only)
  • Our product files for marketing purposes
  • Local system performance metrics

5. How We Use Information

Store Operations:

  • Process your digital product purchases
  • Provide download access and customer support
  • Send purchase confirmations and important updates

Pinterest Marketing:

  • Promote our financial wellness products
  • Create engaging Pinterest content
  • Grow our community of financial wellness enthusiasts

6. Data Security

Creative Mind Prints protects your information through:

  • Secure Payment Processing: We use trusted payment processors (Stripe/PayPal)
  • Local Bot Processing: Pinterest automation runs locally, not on external servers
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what's necessary for purchases
  • Encrypted Storage: All sensitive data stored securely

7. Third-Party Services

We work with these trusted partners:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for secure transactions
  • Email Service: For sending purchase confirmations and support
  • Pinterest API: For our marketing automation (business account only)

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data
  • Download Access: Lifetime access to purchased products

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal tracking:

  • Essential cookies for store functionality
  • Basic analytics to improve user experience
  • No invasive tracking or advertising cookies

10. Children's Privacy

Our financial planning tools are designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. International Users

We serve customers worldwide. Our Pinterest bot operates locally regardless of location. All transactions are processed securely through our payment partners.

12. Changes to This Policy

Creative Mind Prints may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with updated dates.
