1. About Creative Mind Prints

Creative Mind Prints is a financial wellness business dedicated to making budgeting accessible, beautiful, and empowering. We create printable budget planners, debt trackers, savings tools, and expense tracking printables to help people take control of their finances with confidence.

2. Information We Collect

When you shop with Creative Mind Prints, we collect:

  • Purchase Information: Name, email address for delivery of digital products
  • Payment Data: Processed securely by our payment partners (never stored by us)
  • Download Activity: When and which products you access
  • Communication: Messages you send us for support
  • Website Analytics: Basic usage data to improve our store experience

3. How We Use Your Information

Creative Mind Prints uses your information to:

  • Process Purchases: Complete transactions and deliver your financial printables
  • Provide Support: Help with downloads, usage questions, and technical issues
  • Send Updates: Important information about your purchases and new products
  • Improve Experience: Enhance our store and product offerings

4. Information We DON'T Collect

We respect your privacy and do not collect:

  • Personal financial information or account details
  • Social security numbers or tax information
  • Information from other financial apps or services
  • Detailed browsing history or personal data
  • Information about your actual financial situation

5. Data Security & Storage

Your Security Matters: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
  • Secure Payment Processing: All payments handled by trusted processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Encrypted Data Storage: Personal information stored securely with encryption
  • Limited Access: Only authorized Creative Mind Prints staff can access customer data
  • Regular Security Updates: Continuous monitoring and security improvements

6. Third-Party Services

Creative Mind Prints partners with trusted services:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for secure transactions
  • Email Service: For sending purchase confirmations and support
  • Analytics: Basic website analytics to improve user experience
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages for reliable, fast website hosting

7. Your Rights & Control

As a Creative Mind Prints customer, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information with us
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data (note: may affect access to purchases)
  • Lifetime Access: Continue accessing your purchased printables indefinitely
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails anytime

8. Cookies & Website Data

Our Creative Mind Prints website uses:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for store functionality and checkout
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how to improve our store
  • No Advertising Tracking: We don't use invasive advertising cookies

9. Email Communications

We send emails for:

  • Purchase confirmations and download links
  • Customer support responses
  • Important updates about your purchases
  • Optional promotional emails (you can unsubscribe anytime)

10. Children's Privacy

Our financial planning tools are designed for adults managing their finances. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. International Customers

Creative Mind Prints serves customers worldwide. We comply with international privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA. All transactions are processed securely regardless of your location.

12. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Required for providing lifetime product access
  • Necessary for legal or business purposes
  • You haven't requested deletion

13. Changes to This Policy

Creative Mind Prints may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date
  • Significant changes will be emailed to customers
  • Continued use of our store constitutes acceptance
